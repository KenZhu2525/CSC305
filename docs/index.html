About Campus Canvas

Campus Canvas is a community-driven mobile app with a unique twist: users must physically visit specific real-life locations to place virtual pixels based on their real-world location. This app draws inspiration from Reddit, particularly r/Place, where a vast canvas allows users to add a single pixel every few minutes.

Mobile App Documentation

1. Introduction

Welcome to the documentation for our mobile app! This document will guide you through the features and functionalities of the app.

Target Audience

The app is tailored for the campus community, including students, faculty, and staff members. Its primary audience comprises:

  • Students: Enrolled individuals seeking an interactive and engaging platform to connect, collaborate, and access campus-related information.
  • Faculty: Instructors and academic staff aiming to facilitate communication, share resources, and organize campus-related activities.
  • Guests: Family members or friends, even those that are just passing through, have the opportunity to explore the campus in an exciting way.

This app serves as a centralized hub for campus interaction, fostering communication, collaboration, and the sharing of campus-related information among its diverse users.

System Requirements

The app is compatible with devices running Android OS version 7.0 (Nougat) and above.

To ensure optimal performance and functionality, it's recommended to have:

  • Device: Smartphones or tablets with at least 2GB of RAM for smooth operation.
  • Storage: Minimum of 100MB of free storage space to install and run the app.
  • Internet Connection: Access to a stable internet connection for app functionality, updates, and content synchronization.
  • Permissions: Appropriate permissions for accessing location services (if applicable), camera (if required), and other relevant device features for enhanced functionality.

For the best user experience, ensure that your device meets these minimum system requirements and maintains a stable internet connection for uninterrupted access to app features and updates.

  2. Tap on the "Log In" button on the home screen.

4. Pixel Screen / Usage Instructions

Key Features

  • Place a pixel at the location of where the user is at that very moment
  • Choose a desired color for you pixel
  • Toggle the map overlay for proper pixel placement
  • Settings screen that allows user to view important information about account

Example Usage

  1. Walk to destination where user wants to start their creation
  2. Toggle map overlay to 100%, 50%, or 0% to achieve proper pixel placement.
  3. Choose a desired color ranging from 24 different options
  4. Hit place pixel and watch in real time as the color you choose is plotted onto the canvas.
  5. User must wait five minutes before next pixel placement.

5. Troubleshooting

  1. Restart the App: Close the app completely and then reopen it. This can often resolve minor glitches.
  2. Check for Updates: Ensure your app is updated to the latest version, as updates often include bug fixes and performance improvements.
  3. Restart Your Device: Sometimes, the issue might not be with the app but with your device. Restarting your device can clear up any temporary conflicts.
  4. Check Your Internet Connection: A stable internet connection is crucial for many apps to function correctly. Verify your Wi-Fi or cellular data connection.
  5. Clear Cache/Data: Go to your device's settings, find the app, and clear its cache. If this doesn't work, try clearing the app's data, but be aware this may delete saved information.
  6. Reinstall the App: Uninstall the app and then reinstall it from the app store. This can resolve issues caused by a corrupt installation.
  7. Check Device Storage: Insufficient storage space can hinder app performance. Ensure your device has enough free space.
  8. Contact Us: If the problem persists, fill out the feedback form. Provide details about the issue, the steps you've already taken, and your device model and OS version for more efficient assistance.
